You’re in the right place if…

Some of this might feel familiar

There’s no “right” reason to start therapy. If any of this lands, it’s worth a conversation.

Anxiety that won’t switch off

Racing thoughts, tight chest, overthinking, or feeling on-edge even when nothing’s wrong.

The weight of past trauma

Difficult or unsafe experiences, abuse, or memories that still shape how you feel today.

Relationships that hurt

Intimate partner violence, boundaries, family pressure, or feeling unseen and unsafe.

A low mood that lingers

Sadness, numbness, exhaustion, or losing interest in things that used to matter to you.

Big or uncertain transitions

Identity, coming out, moving, grief, or simply not feeling like yourself lately.

“Is therapy even for me?”

You don’t need a diagnosis or a crisis. Curiosity is enough. Let’s just talk first.

Questions, answered

Before you reach out

Is everything I say confidential?

Yes. What you share stays between us, within the standard ethical limits (for example, if there’s a serious risk to your safety or someone else’s). We’ll always be transparent about this.

What if I feel awkward or don’t know what to say?

That’s completely normal. Most people feel that way at first. You don’t need to prepare anything or “perform.” We’ll start wherever you are, and silence is okay too.

Does online therapy actually work?

Yes. Research shows online therapy is as effective as in-person for most concerns, with the added comfort of being in your own space. All you need is a private spot and a stable connection.

Which languages can we talk in?

We can work in English, हिन्दी (Hindi), বাংলা (Bengali) or অসমীয়া (Assamese), whichever you feel most yourself in. You’re warmly welcome in any of them.

Individual or group: which is right for me?

Individual sessions offer fully private, focused 1:1 support. Groups offer connection and shared understanding with others. Not sure? We’ll figure it out together on the free intake call.

What happens on the free intake call?

It’s a relaxed 15-minute chat. You share a little about what’s bringing you here, ask us anything, and we see if it feels right. There’s no obligation to book after it.
