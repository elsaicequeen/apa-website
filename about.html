My story

Why I do this work

Before private practice, I worked closely with survivors of gender and sexual violence as a child counsellor, and on research building community mental-health care and ending violence against children. That work shaped everything about how I sit with people today: gently, without judgement, and with deep respect for what they’ve survived.

I believe healing means feeling safe enough to be honest, supported enough to face hard things, and reminded that your story makes sense. My role is to walk alongside you at your own pace.

What we stand for

Our values

Safety first

You set the pace. Nothing is forced, and you never have to share more than you’re ready to.

Everyone belongs

All genders, LGBTQ+ folks, every faith, caste and background are genuinely welcome here.

Context matters

Your struggles don’t exist in a vacuum. We take your circumstances and systems seriously.

Plain honesty

No jargon, no gatekeeping. If we’re not the right fit, we’ll help you find who is.

Your language

We can work in English, हिन्दी, বাংলা or অসমীয়া, whichever you feel most yourself in.

Real-world tools
