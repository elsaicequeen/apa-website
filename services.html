How I help

Support that meets you where you are

Whatever brought you here, the goal is the same: to help you feel safer, steadier and more like yourself. Here are some of the areas I work with most.

Trauma & PTSD

I help you process painful or unsafe experiences gently, so the past loosens its grip on your present.

Anxiety & stress

Tools to quiet the racing thoughts and the constant bracing, so you can breathe a little easier.

Depression & low mood

Support to move through the heaviness and slowly reconnect with what matters to you.

Abuse & intimate partner violence

A safe, believing space for survivors, at your pace, with your safety always first.

Women’s mental health

Space for the pressures, expectations and experiences that come with navigating the world as a woman.

Identity & life transitions

Coming out, big changes, grief and self-worth, with affirming support as you figure out who you’re becoming.

Working with something not listed here? Reach out anyway, and there’s a good chance I can help or point you in the right direction.
